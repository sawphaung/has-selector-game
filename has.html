:has() lab

Learn CSS's most powerful selector by playing — write real :has() and watch the right elements light up.

:has() is the relational selector: it styles an element based on what it contains. .card:has(img) means "a card that holds an image." It's the parent selector CSS never had — and it can even reach across siblings.
