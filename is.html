:is() garden

Learn CSS's grouping selector by playing — write real :is() and watch the right plants light up.

:is() is the grouping selector: it matches an element that fits any selector in its list. :is(h1, h2, h3) a replaces three selectors with one — and when it sits on both sides of a combinator, it multiplies: :is(header, aside) :is(h2, h3) covers four combinations in one line.
